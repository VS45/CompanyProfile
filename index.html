About VS45 Technologies Limited

VS45 Technologies Limited, headquartered on the last floor of Abege Plaza at No 50 JS Tarka Way in Gboko, is a pioneering technology firm established on October 3rd, 2019. As a company at the forefront of the tech industry, VS45 Technologies is dedicated to providing cutting-edge solutions through its comprehensive suite of services designed to meet the needs of both individuals and enterprises. Our services include:

      Web Design Training: Focused on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and user experience, tailored for those entering the field or enhancing their skills.
      Web Development Training**: Covers a range of technologies from front-end to back-end, including React and Node.js, for creating sophisticated web applications.
      Full Stack Development**: Integrates advanced programming languages like Go, Node.js, and Java Spring Boot with front-end frameworks such as Angular, React, and Next.js, offering a complete understanding of both server and client-side development.
      Mobile App Development Training**: Utilizes the React Native framework to teach how to develop cross-platform mobile applications efficiently.
      Front End Development Training**: Focuses on creating responsive and dynamic web interfaces using the React library and Angular framework.

      Enterprise Software Solutions**:

      Provides customized software applications designed to streamline operations, enhance efficiency, and foster growth in large organizations.

Located in a strategic area of Gboko, VS45 Technologies leverages its state-of-the-art facilities and expert instructors with real-world industry experience to empower clients and trainees, preparing them for challenges in the fast-evolving tech landscape. Whether providing training for future tech professionals or delivering enterprise solutions, VS45 Technologies is committed to innovation and excellence, ensuring clients and students alike are equipped to meet the demands of the digital age.

Web Design Training

Web Design Training is a key service offered by our company, designed to empower individuals with the skills and knowledge necessary to create functional, aesthetically pleasing websites. This training covers a broad range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and user experience design, ensuring participants can handle both the technical and design aspects of web development. The course is suitable for beginners interested in entering the field as well as for professionals looking to enhance their skill set. With hands-on projects and real-world applications, trainees will learn to design responsive and interactive websites, focusing on best practices and the latest web standards. This program not only boosts career opportunities by adding a crucial skill to participants' portfolios but also equips them to contribute effectively to any project or role in the digital economy.

Web Development

Web Development Training is a comprehensive service offered by our company aimed at equipping participants with the essential skills and knowledge needed to build robust and scalable web applications. This training encompasses both front-end and back-end technologies, including frameworks like React for the client-side and Node.js for server-side development. Participants will also learn database management with systems like MySQL and MongoDB, ensuring they can manage data effectively. The course structure is designed to cater to both beginners and seasoned developers looking to upgrade their skills, with a focus on coding practices, debugging, and deploying web applications. By participating in this program, individuals will gain the ability to create full-stack web solutions, crucial for careers in software development and technology. Hands-on projects during the course allow learners to apply their knowledge in real-world scenarios, thereby enhancing their problem-solving skills and preparing them for challenges in the tech industry.

Full Stack Development

Full Stack Development training is a premier service provided by our company, designed to develop skilled developers capable of handling both the front-end and back-end components of web applications. This course offers an in-depth exploration of advanced programming languages and frameworks that are pivotal in today’s tech landscape, including Go, Node.js, and Java Spring Boot for server-side development, coupled with front-end frameworks such as Angular, React, and Next.js. The curriculum is structured to ensure participants gain comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience across all layers of software development. Trainees will learn to create efficient, scalable back-end services with Go and Java Spring Boot, and dynamic, user-friendly front-end interfaces using Angular, React, and Next.js. This holistic approach not only amplifies their versatility but also enhances their employability by mastering the full spectrum of web development tools and practices. By the end of the program, participants will be capable of designing and implementing complete web applications from scratch, making them invaluable assets in the fast-paced field of software development. This training is perfect for individuals aiming to become proficient full-stack developers and lead complex projects in their future careers.

Mobile App Development

Mobile App Development Training at our company is specifically tailored to equip participants with the skills to build high-performance mobile applications using the React Native framework. This training focuses on teaching comprehensive React Native concepts, enabling trainees to create cross-platform apps that operate smoothly on both iOS and Android devices from a single codebase. The course covers essential topics such as component-based architecture, state management, and integration with third-party services and APIs. Trainees will also delve into advanced features like navigation, handling gestures, and animations, ensuring they can develop interactive and user-friendly mobile interfaces. Participants will benefit from hands-on sessions where they apply their learning to build functional mobile applications, thereby gaining practical experience and troubleshooting skills. This training is ideal for developers interested in expanding their skill set into the mobile domain, offering them a competitive edge in the job market by mastering one of the most popular and versatile mobile development frameworks available today.

Front End Development

Front End Development Training at our company is designed to equip participants with cutting-edge skills in building modern, responsive web interfaces using the React library and Angular framework. This specialized training program focuses on the key functionalities and best practices of both technologies, enabling developers to create interactive and efficient web applications. Participants will delve into React, exploring its component-based architecture, JSX syntax, state management with hooks, and context for managing app-wide data. React’s ecosystem, including popular libraries such as Redux and React Router, will also be covered, providing learners with a comprehensive toolkit for building complex single-page applications. Simultaneously, the course will cover Angular, offering insights into its powerful features like TypeScript integration, dependency injection, and the modular architecture that Angular uses to organize applications effectively. Trainees will learn to leverage Angular’s robust framework to build scalable and maintainable front-end applications. This training is hands-on, with numerous projects and real-world scenarios that help solidify the concepts taught. It's perfect for aspiring front-end developers or seasoned professionals looking to master these predominant technologies in the web development landscape. Participants will leave the program with the skills necessary to contribute to any front-end development team immediately, using some of the industry’s most demanded frameworks.
